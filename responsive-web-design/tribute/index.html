Žydrūnas 'Big Z' Savickas

History's Strongest Man

Biography

The future strongman was born in the mid-70s of the last century on the outskirts of Lithuania, in the city of Bizai. From childhood, the boy was distinguished by a strong, muscular physique. He always liked sports: already in adolescence, under the influence of television programs, he decided to become a professional athlete.
